Clonmel Pride

About us...

Clonmel Pride was founded in 2019 with the purpose of bringing celebreation, awareness and acceptance of the LGBTQ+ community to the town of Clonmel and it's surrounding areas.

Our Mission Statement

Clonmel Pride visualises an open and safe community for all. We aim to provide the opportunity to celebrate diversity, love and fellowship within our community. We strive to promote unity, inclusion and awareness of sexual and gender diversity and aim to create a sense of community and a safe space for LGBTQ+ individuals, their families and their allies.
